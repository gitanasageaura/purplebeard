yoga

Yoga is a path for union which means "that which brings you reality".

This is often practiced in the form of a series of slow, meaningful and intentional movements that mimic and resonate with forms found in nature.

There are four paths of yoga; Karma, Kriya, Bhakati and Gnana.

The most common types of yoga are; Hatha yoga, Ashtanga yoga, Kundalini yoga, Restorative yoga and Yin and Yang yoga.

There are of course many more types to explore, you may find more information below to find a form that suits your present needs.

hatha yoga

A great way to to sync your mind and body whilst you are enjoying the natural surroundings. This is a great alternative for those who find sitting mediation difficult, as you can still achieve the same mind body awareness whilst moving.
